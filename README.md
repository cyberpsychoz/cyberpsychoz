## Why teams hire me

- **I understand both AI and backend engineering.** I can work with models, but I also know that the model is only one organ in the beast. APIs, data, queues, logs, configs, fallbacks, tests — that is where the real system lives.
- **I care about production quality.** A beautiful notebook is nice; a service that works after deployment is better. Revolutionary thought, I know.
- **I can move from research to implementation.** I enjoy experiments, but I do not worship them. If something works, I package it. If it does not, I measure it, fix it, or throw it into the swamp where bad prototypes belong.
- **I communicate clearly.** I write documentation, explain trade-offs, and try to make technical decisions understandable for both engineers and business people.
